democratizing science

The elements project is opening up MIT Media Lab research in the form of STEAM learning programs that democratize science in three elemental areas: Earth, Ocean, and Space.

LEGO Underwater Robots

Combining LEGO, robotics, and seawater into a playground of project-based learning and citizen science for budding engineers and explorers.

Personal Food Computer EDU

A tabletop-sized, controlled environment agriculture technology platform designed with educators and children in mind.

Climate CubeSat Co-Building

A program for young people to design, build, test, and launch a CubeSat that makes climate science and earth-observation measurements.
